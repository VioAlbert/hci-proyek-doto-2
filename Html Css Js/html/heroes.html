Choose Your Hero
Doto 2 hero pool is diverse. From sneaky shooters to sacred spirits, choose one of them and unleash their abilities in the battlefield!
Wei, the Chaos Lord
Previously a fierce warrior, Wei is able to give his enemies a hard time. With his mighty spear, he controls his enemies' positioning to his favor. After all is set, he summons meteors and reduce them to dust.
Skill 1: Chaos Spear (Passive)
Wei strengthens his spear, raising his attack speed and damage.
Skill 2: Binding Order (Active)
Wei stabs his spear to the ground, slowing enemies in the area.
Skill 3: Controller's Wish (Active)
Wei throws his spear, pulling enemies around to it.
Skill 4: Heaven's Punishment (Active)
Wei summons meteors, dealing physical AOE damage.
Hisui, the Guardian Spirit
Hisui is a guardian of the spirit gates. Entrusted by Jade Emperor, he is able to freely summon the gates, open it, and release the spirits contained. The spirits, in return, will help him fight his enemies.
Skill 1: Spirit Summon (Active)
Hisui opens a small gate, letting one spirit out and do magic damage in a line area
Skill 2: Guardian's Trust (Passive)
Everytime a spirit hits an enemy, Spirit Summon cooldown reduces a bit.
Skill 3: Spirit Gate (Active)
Hisui summons a binding gate. Any enemies going through the gate will summon a spirit who will do magic damace.
Skill 4: Gate of Another (Active)
Hisui summons a giant gate, letting many spirits out. The spirits then circle Hisui whilst doing magic damage.
Xon, the Amethyst Knight
A brave knight in the past, Xon is a cyborg built to fight in wars. His enchanted amethyst sword is capable of dealing great damage. He is never afraid to charge and jump into the battle. He is programmed to raise his shield in case of any dangers.
Skill 1: Charge (Active)
Xon charges in a straight line, pushing any enemies in front of him.
Skill 2: Knight Jump (Active)
Xon jumps into an area, slowing and physically damaging the enemy.
Skill 3: Enchanted Amethyst (Passive)
Xon enchants his sword, increasing his physical damage greatly.
Skill 4: Autonomous Shield (Passive)
Xon is able to block and reduce any incoming basic and physical attacks in a certain chance.
Pagas, the Ice Demon
Pagas is a demon who controls the icy northern Atmonos. An archenemy of Hikuma, Pagas is capable of freezing her enemies to death. Waving her wand, Pagas brings the land sheer cold, suffocating her enemies slowly.
Skill 1: Frostbite (Active)
In a conical area, Pagas freezes the land and deal magical damage.
Skill 2: Feel The Breeze (Passive)
All enemies within the freezed land will be slowed and magical damage overtime.
Skill 3: Mana Recharge (Passive)
Pagas receives a certain amount of mana per second.
Skill 4: Sheer cold (Active)
Pagas waves her wand, freezing the area around her and dealing magical damage.
Reid & Gleiphr, the Scavengers
Reid & Gleiphr are a hunter and a beast who were banished by her village. Reid & Gleiphr shares a close bond, enabling them to deal damage to different enemies at the same time. While Reid has her glaive mastery, Gleiphr can roar to intimidate her enemies.
Skill 1: Pounce (Active)
Gleiphr jumps to the front.
Skill 2: Glaive mastery (Passive)
Reid's Glaive can bounce a number of times between enemies.
Skill 3: Roar (Active)
Gleiphr roars, slowing the enemy heroes.
Skill 4: One Mind (Active)
In a certain duration, Reid & Gleiphr can both attack different enemies at the same time.
Ichika, the Ranger
Ichika has trained kyudo for her entire life. Her senses allows accurate pinpoint of her enemies location in the area. Using her trusty bow, she can either rapid fire or launch a powerful strike to finish off her foes.
Skill 1: Hunter Senses (Passive)
Ichika can sense enemies from a certain radius.
Skill 2: Raining Arrows (Active)
Ichika's attack speed increases temporarily, making her shots faster.
Skill 3: Sharpshooter (Passive)
Having trained for years, Ichika can launch arrows accurately and give occasional critical hits.
Skill 4: Charged Shot (Active)
Ichika goes into a stance to charge her arrows. Afterwards, she shoots a powerful attack in a straight line.
Oliver, the Wizard
Oliver was a guard mage in a kingdom destroyed long time ago. Currently living a nomadic life, He has taught himself to do wizardry spells. From mana explosions to elemental beams, Oliver will eliminate his foes!
Skill 1: Elemental Sphere (Active)
Oliver chants and casts an elemental sphere and send it frontwards, dealing magic damage along the way.
Skill 2: Mana Explosion (Active)
Oliver chants and casts an explosion and gives effect to enemies caught in it. After a certain duration, the enemies will take damage based on the mana they consumed.
Skill 3: Spell Fluency (Passive)
Oliver chants each spells quicker.
Skill 4: Obliteration (Active)
Oliver chants for a while, unleashing a large elemental beam afterwards.
Hikuma, the Fire Demon
Hikuma is a demon who controls the fiery southern Atmonos. An archenemy of Pagas, Hikuma is capable of burning the lands around him. When in need, he can summon his horse that will enhance his skills further.
Skill 1: Flaming Land (Toggleable)
Hikuma will burn the area around him, damaging any enemies or creeps.
Skill 2: Burn, Burn, Burn! (Passive)
Hikuma enhances his flames' physical damage and burning damage.
Skill 3: Hot Breath (Active)
Hikuma breathes flame in a cone area, dealing physical damage.
Skill 4: Come Forth! (Active)
Hikuma summons his horse and rides it. While on it, Hikuma will have his burning damage and AOE boosted.
