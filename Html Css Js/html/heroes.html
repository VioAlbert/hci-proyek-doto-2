Choose Your Hero
Doto 2 hero pool is diverse. From sneaky shooters to sacred spirits, choose one of them and unleash their abilities in the battlefield!
Wei, the Chaos Lord
Previously a fierce warrior, Wei is able to give his enemies a hard time. With his mighty spear, he controls his enemies' positioning to his favor. After all is set, he summons meteors and reduce them to dust.
Skill 1: Chaos Spear (Passive)
Wei strengthens his spear, raising his attack speed and damage.
Skill 2: Binding Order (Active)
Wei stabs his spear to the ground, slowing enemies in the area.
Skill 3: Controller's Wish (Active)
Wei throws his spear, pulling enemies around to it.
Skill 4: Heaven's Punishment (Active)
Wei summons meteors, dealing physical AOE damage.

Ichika, the Ranger
Ichika has trained kyudo for her entire life. Her senses allows accurate pinpoint of her enemies location in the area. Using her trusty bow, she can either rapid fire or launch a powerful strike to finish off her foes.
Skill 1: Hunter Senses (Passive)
Ichika can sense enemies from a certain radius.
Skill 2: Raining Arrows (Active)
Ichika's attack speed increases temporarily, making her shots faster.
Skill 3: Sharpshooter (Passive)
Having trained for years, Ichika can launch arrows accurately and give occasional critical hits.
Skill 4: Charged Shot (Active)
Ichika goes into a stance to charge her arrows. Afterwards, she shoots a powerful attack in a straight line.
